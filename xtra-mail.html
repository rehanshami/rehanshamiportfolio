Xtra Mail journey redesign - Spark NZ

As the business transitioned to providing Xtra Mail as a subscription-based email service, we concluded that a complete overhaul of all Xtra Mail online interfaces was necessary. My responsibilities included developing a new landing page and establishing supplementary pages to communicate the impending changes.

The project

In the process of transitioning Xtra Mail to a subscription-based service, we initially carried out extensive user testing and customer interviews to gain a deeper understanding of our customer base. We discovered that our customers highly valued their email service, viewing it as an integral part of their online identity. Therefore, it was crucial for us to design an exceptional online customer experience for this new journey, while also effectively communicating the forthcoming changes to our customers.

Duration: 1 month

Methods: Scoping content, wireframes, image editing, researching, SEO, content writing, and creating customer journey.

Tools: Adobe AEM, Adobe Photoshop, and Figma.
