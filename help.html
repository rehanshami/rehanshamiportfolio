The project

In the process of revamping our Xtra Mail experiences, we chose to prioritize our support materials. We made sure to present technical concepts in a way that’s easy for customers to understand, and we aimed to make the content widely accessible.

Duration: 2 months

Methods: Scoping content, wireframes, image editing, researching, SEO, and content rewriting.

Tools: Adobe AEM, Adobe Photoshop, and Figma.

Challenges, insights, and outcomes

Our research on the users of our help content revealed that the majority of our audience belonged to a senior age group. My role involved translating the content into a non-technical language for easier comprehension. Additionally, I pioneered the use of GIFs as a visual aid to enhance our users’ understanding.
