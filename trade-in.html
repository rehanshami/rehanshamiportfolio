Trade in and save - Spark NZ

The “Trade in and save” project entailed the development of a new online pathway for customers to instantly receive an estimated trade-in value for their mobile device. This platform also guided customers to their nearest Spark store for the trade-in process. My role encompassed the creation of the online page and the digital assets.

The project

In this project, my responsibilities encompassed constructing the trade-in webpage, creating the imagery, and ensuring that the webpage was optimized for accessibility, SEO, and responsive design.

Duration: 1.5 months

Methods: Scoping content, wireframes, image editing, researching, and content writing.

Tools: Adobe AEM, Adobe Photoshop, and Figma.

Context

In order to maintain our competitiveness in the market, the Mobile team decided to implement an online trade-in process for our customers. Given the impending brownout period, we had a tight deadline of less than two months to launch our website.

I worked in close collaboration with UX designers, CX designers, REACT developers, AEM developers, and stakeholders from the Mobile team to achieve the final result.

Challenges, insights, and outcomes

The project made use of our newly introduced SPA templates and necessitated the construction of an estimation tool. Initially, the tool was plagued with numerous bugs. I offered consistent and ongoing feedback to the React and AEM developers to address these issues.
